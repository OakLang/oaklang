# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: 3 October, 2024_

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our software, in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our software:

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your username and email address.
- **Usage Data**: We collect data related to how you interact with the software, such as usage patterns, features used, and non-personal statistics.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the software.
- Analyze usage to improve performance and user experience.
- Generate aggregated insights, reports, and content for internal or external purposes, including public blog posts or videos.
- Contact you personally regarding your experience or updates related to your account.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with third parties unless:

Required by law.
You have given explicit consent.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research or analysis purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify individuals.

## 4. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for as long as necessary to provide access to the software, meet legal requirements, or resolve disputes.

## 5. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Ask for updates or corrections to your data.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal data, except where legally required.
- **Objection**: Object to certain uses of your data, such as non-essential communications.
- **Portability**: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

## 6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, but no system can guarantee complete security. We cannot be held liable for unauthorized access or breaches beyond our control.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on our site or software interface.

## 8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at concact@oaklang.com.
